# PokéClicker Scripts Reborn

A reboot of the infamous [PokéClicker Scripts](https://github.com/ephymew/Pokeclicker-Scripts) project.

I have decided that I will be returning to work on quality of life and enhancements for this game again. However, I will be working on this on the side of things. I wouldn't expect much here for a while, but we'll see when I drop the first release.

I have plans for the reboot of this project, and how I wish to go about it as well. Here are the current plans, and also some notes as well to keep in mind.

- I will be working on this myself only. There will be no PRs.
- I am not returning to the original project, and I am no longer providing support for any old scripts.
- I am starting from square one and from scratch here. I plan to rewrite a majority of what I've made previously.
- Reborn may or may not be compaitable with any of the older scripts. Reborn will not be developed with the idea of ensuring compaitability with any other scripts/automation.
- There will be no scripts here in PSR, my plan is and will only be an AIO unified script that modualarly functions.
- The only time I am going to "fix" or updaate any old scripts is if I choose to take any of the old ones and port them over to Reborn.
- Some old scripts and or features of them will be omitted here in Reborn.
- That is also to say that there will surely be new things as well.

And as a disclaimer, I have not kept up with or have been invested in Pokeclicker for years. So, there's a lot of work to be done.
